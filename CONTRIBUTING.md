# Contributing

This repository is open source (MIT OR Apache-2.0), but contributions are accepted only from
approved contributors.

## Policy

- We do not accept unsolicited pull requests.
- We may close PRs from non-approved contributors without review.
- Forks are permitted under the license, but we do not provide support for downstream forks.

## How to contribute (approved contributors)

1. Discuss the change with the maintainers before writing code.
2. Keep changes minimal and focused; preserve determinism and correctness contracts.
3. Ensure CI passes (`cargo check --all-targets --all-features` and `cargo build --release --all-features`).

## Licensing of contributions

By submitting a contribution to this repository, you agree that it may be redistributed under the
project license terms (MIT OR Apache-2.0).
